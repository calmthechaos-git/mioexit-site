Questions

Straight answers.

How long does a migration take?

Depends on library size. A 500-video library typically takes 2-3 days. Larger libraries (2,000+) take about a week. The audit itself takes 24 hours.

Will my members lose access during migration?

No. We run the migration in parallel — your MIO account stays fully active until you're ready to switch. Zero downtime.

Where does my content go?

Wherever you want. Most clients choose BunnyCDN (fast, global, ~$13/mo for terabytes of video) but we can deliver to S3, Cloudflare, your own servers, or any storage you prefer. You get standard MP4 files — they work everywhere.

Can I still use my existing membership platform after migration?

Yes. Your videos become standard embeddable files that work with any platform — WordPress, Kajabi, Circle, custom sites, or anything that accepts iframe embeds. You're just hosting them on your terms instead of MIO's.

What about my course structure and playlists?

Preserved completely. We recreate your hub/folder hierarchy and document every playlist with exact file ordering. Nothing is lost. You get a complete map of your entire library.

What if something goes wrong?

Your original content stays on MIO untouched throughout the migration. We copy — we never delete. You only cancel MIO when you've verified everything is exactly where you want it.

Do you need my Membership.io login?

We need admin access to catalog and extract your content — but you never share your password. If you're on MIO's Grow or Scale plan, you simply invite us as a team member. We get our own login, you remove us the moment migration is complete. If you're on the Start plan (which doesn't support team members), we'll send you a secure one-time link to share credentials — it self-destructs after we view it, and you change your password when we're done. Either way, we never touch your billing, delete anything, or modify your live content.

Why can't I just do this myself?

Technically, you can — the same way you could move houses without a moving company. MIO doesn't give you a bulk export. There's no "download everything" button. You'd be downloading files one at a time, rebuilding your folder structure from memory, and hoping you didn't miss anything. We spent months figuring out how to do this properly so you don't have to.

What happens to my content if I cancel Membership.io without exporting first?

It's gone. According to Membership.io's own help docs, data cannot be retrieved once your account is cancelled. That's why you need to export everything before you cancel — and that's exactly what our service does. We make sure every file is safely in your hands before you flip the switch.

I'm on Searchie, not Membership.io — can you still help?

Same platform, different name. Searchie rebranded to Membership.io in 2024. Our tools work with both. If your account is at app.membership.io or app.searchie.io, we've got you covered.

Can you migrate me to Kajabi, Circle, or Skool?

We handle the extraction — getting your content out of MIO and into your hands as standard files. From there, you can upload to any platform you choose. We can also deliver directly to video hosting (BunnyCDN, S3, Cloudflare) if you want to self-host and embed into whatever membership platform you land on.

How much does the migration service cost?

Fixed pricing based on library size: $497 for up to 200 videos, $997 for 200–1,000 videos, $1,997 for 1,000–5,000 videos. No hourly billing, no surprises. Libraries over 5,000 videos get custom pricing. See full pricing details.
