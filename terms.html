1. Service Description

MIOExit ("we", "us", "our") provides a done-for-you content extraction and migration service for Membership.io (formerly Searchie) users. We extract videos, files, playlists, folder structures, and member data from your Membership.io account and deliver them to the hosting provider of your choice.

We provide content extraction only. We do not build, configure, or manage membership platforms, websites, or course delivery systems. We do not provide ongoing hosting.

2. Account Access

To perform the migration, we require temporary admin-level access to your Membership.io account. Access is granted through one of two methods:

  • Team member invite (Grow and Scale plans): You invite us as a team member. We receive our own login. You remove us when migration is complete.
  • Secure credential sharing (Start plan): We use a one-time, self-destructing link to receive your credentials. You change your password when migration is complete.

We will never access your billing settings, delete any content from your Membership.io account, or modify your live content. We copy — we never alter or remove.

4. The "Every Single File" Guarantee

We guarantee extraction of every file in your Membership.io account that has a valid, accessible source URL. If any extractable file is missing after migration, we will re-run the migration at no additional cost.

Exclusions: Some files in Membership.io accounts may not have valid source URLs — these are typically failed uploads, unprocessed recordings, or files that never completed transcoding. We will identify these files in your audit report and verification report. These files are excluded from the guarantee because they cannot be extracted by any method.

The guarantee is valid for 60 days from the date we deliver your verification report.
